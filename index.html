Our story

Born in a traffic jam
on NH-65

tripride started when our founder was stuck in bumper-to-bumper traffic between Hyderabad and Tirupati — and realised that three empty seats next to him could have carried three people making the exact same journey.

We built tripride to solve India's intercity mobility gap: buses are full, trains are booked, and taxis are expensive. Carpooling is the natural answer — but it needed a trustworthy platform.

Today, tripride operates across South India with a growing community of verified drivers and passengers who share not just rides, but stories.

Safety first
Every user is ID-verified. SOS, live tracking, and trusted contacts built-in.
Zero profiteering
Drivers share actual costs — no surge pricing, no hidden commissions.
Community over platform
Built on trust, not algorithms. Ratings are earned, not bought.
